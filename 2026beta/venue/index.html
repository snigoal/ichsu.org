Venue

Conference Venue

Numisheet 2025 takes place at the 4-Star-Superior Leonardo Royal Hotel Munich which is located in the northern part of Munich.

With its modern conference rooms, the Leonardo Royal Hotel offers sufficient space for our conference. The session rooms are equipped with excellent sound and presentation technology and most of the session rooms have natural lighting.
A spacious conference foyer and a seated garden terrace are available for coffee and lunch breaks.

The conference venue is located right next to the Olympiapark and the city center Marienplatz can be reached by subway in 13 minutes.

For room booking at the leonardo hotel please go to:
https://www.leonardo-hotels.com/munich/leonardo-royal-hotel-munich

Journey to the Venue
By Public Transport

Subway line U3, stop “Oberwiesenfeld”, Exit D

From the airport take the S1 (S-Bahn) towards Munich until the stop “Moosach”. Change to the subway line U3 direction “Fürstenried-West”. Get off at the third stop “Oberwiesenfeld”. Take Exit D to the street. You reach the hotel after approximatly 100 m.

From the main station take the subway line U2 direction “Feldmoching”. At stop “Scheidplatz” change to the subway line U3 direction “Moosach”. Get off at the third stop “Oberwiesenfeld”. Take Exit D to the street where the hotel is located approximatly 100 m away.

More information can be found on the Website of MVG Munich Transport.

by car

Address for your navigation system:
Moosacher Strasse 90, 80809 Munich

Parking is possible in the hotel underground parking for € 19 per day. Reservation is not possible.
