Materials, Processes and Equipment

August 21-23, 2024, Hefei, China

Conference Topics

        Authors from all over the world are welcome to participate in this international academic conference. Academic papers accepted at the conference will be published by Inno Science Press. Outstanding papers will have the opportunity to give oral presentations, and the rest will be displayed by posters. Details can be found on the conference website www.ichsu.cn/www.ichsu.org.

        The conference topics is as follows (including but not limited to):

 

  • Hot stamping of high-strength steel/high-strength aluminum alloys

  • Near-zero emission hot stamping materials, processes and equipment

  • Hot stamping process and technical equipment based on industrial AI

  • Cold stamping forming technology of advanced high-strength steel 1180-1470MPa

  • Technology and application of multi-material forming (mixed forming of carbon fiber composite materials, steel and aluminum)

  • Cold/hot stamping technology for multi-component integrated tailor-welded blanks

  • Laser blanking, tailor welding and cutting of advanced high-strength steel

  • Cold stamping and roll forming of advanced high-strength steels

  • High-strength steel pipe thermal expansion technology and equipment technology

  • New coating and forming processes and prevention of hydrogen embrittlement delayed fracture

  • Modeling and forming simulation of high-strength steel hot and cold stamped parts

  • Automotive advanced high-strength steel stamping products and applications

  • New energy vehicle body structure, cold/hot stamping and connection

  • High-strength steel heating and cooling methods and processes

  • Microstructure and phase transition of cold/hot stamping materials

  • Design and manufacturing of high-strength steel cold/hot stamping molds

  • Hot stamping modeling and simulation technology and applications

  • Testing and evaluation of advanced high-strength steel stampings

  • Digital and intelligent technology and application of hot stamping equipment

  • Hot forming die steel and friction and wear testing

  • Research and application of green and environmentally friendly mold lubrication and release agents

  • Other advanced forming technologies and equipment

Instructions for Authors

1. All papers must be original, unpublished, and cannot be submitted to other publications at the same time.

2. A paper must focus on a theme, and be in English. Papers in Chinese won't be accepted.

3. A paper should be within 5 pages, extra fees will be charged for extra pages. Submissions must use the conference paper template. (Ms Word Paper template)

4. Paper submission: (1) Submit directly through the ICHSU2024 website; (2) Submit to official email ichsu@vip.163.com. Please use "ICHSU2022 Paper Submission" as the email title, to avoid acceptance delay of your paper.

Conference Paper Publishing

        Authors from all over the world are welcome to participate in this international academic conference. The conference will collect 150 papers, and 120 selected papers will be presented in oral form. Academic papers accepted at the conference will be published by Atlantis Press (a sub-journal of Springer Nature (Open), indexed by Google and China National Knowledge Infrastructure (CNKI)). For the basic registration fee, each paper should be no longer than 5 pages and follow a template format.
