High performance sheet metalhot stamping materials、processes and equipment

July 2022，China · WuHan

Paper theme

        Authors from all over the world are welcome to participate in this international academic conference.100 papers will be collected by this conference,80 selected papers will be reported orally. After the conference,all accepted papers will be officially published by the International Science Press,details can be viewed on the conference website www.ichsu.cn.

        Topics include but are not limited to:

 

  • New high strength steel/aluminum alloy materials

  • Hot stamping of high-strength steel/high-strength aluminium alloy

  • Cold stamping and rolling forming of high strength steel

  • Cold/hot stamping of tailor-welded blanks

  • Modeling and Forming Simulation of Stamping Parts

  • Advanced high strength steel stamping products and application

  • Material surface condition, friction and lubrication

  • Material coatings, corrosion and corrosion protection

  • Hot stamping part design and body

  • Heating and cooling methods and processes

  • Microstructure and phase transition of cold-hot stamping forming material

  • Die design and manufacturing

  • Test and evaluation of advanced high strength steel stamps

  • Laser cutting and welding of advanced high strength steel stamping parts

  • Digital and intelligent technology and application of hot stamping forming equipment

  • Lightweight design and advanced high strength steel part forming technology for commercial vehicles

  • Other advanced forming technologies

Instructions for submission

1. All papers must be original, unpublished, and cannot be submitted to other publications at the same time.

2. A paper must highlight its theme, and the submitted paper must be in English MS Word format as required by the conference paper template (Chinese papers won't be accepted).

3. You must use the template format developed by the meeting,download it from the website.

4. The paper is submitted by e-mail and sent to ichsu@vip.163.com. Please write "ICHSU2022" in the title of the submission email, so as not to delay the receipt of papers.
