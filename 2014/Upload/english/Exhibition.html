Exhibition Area Activities

The conference set exhibiting area of 1000 square meters, and is expected to 20 booths. The quantity of booths is limited, please be quick if you want.

Exhibition Scope:
  • Hot stamping products;
  • Hot stamping system integrated production line;
  • Equipment including heating equipment, transportation equipment, laser equipment, presses, robots, dies;
  • System and accessories including 3D simulation software, robot system integration, sensor, vision system;
  • Materials including high strength steel, mould steel materials, coating materials, die steel.
