Conference Introduction

The Second International Conference on Advanced High Strength Steel and Press Hardening will be held on October, 2015 in Changsha, China. The conference will be a grand academic convention around the material, processes and equipment research of AHSS after the very successful first meeting on hot stamping of UHSS in Chongqing.

In 2014 the production and marketing in China is still the first throughout the world. China has become the last and biggest automotive market in the world, however energy-saving emission reduction and safety are always the focus during the development of automotive industry. The light-weight of vehicle is the most direct and effective means, and advanced high strength steel and press hardening is efficient and cost-effective technology to realize both the light-weight and assurance of safety. Because of this reason, hot stamping has developed at a breathtaking speed as soon as the technology enters China, and more than 40 production lines have been completed and realized trial production in just a few years. The huge market demand encouraged the development and application of advanced high strength steel and press hardening technology. AHSS and press hardening in China has present great market potential and business opportunities, the technological application in China also emerge as a new trend. China has become the emerging developing base of AHSS and press hardening technology which arouses the interest from academics and industry all over the world.

Objective

Just in this situation, we decided to convene the second international conference on AHSS and press hardening. The conference will provide a platform to the academic and industrial peers for:

  • 1.Academic research achievement exchange for cold forming and press hardening technology of AHSS;
  • 2Application exhibition and exchange of cold forming and press hardening of AHSS;
  • 3.Understanding development in the future about research and application of AHSS and press hardening technology in China and the world;

On this platform, all participants, whether come from technology R&D units, equipment manufacturing units, automotive production enterprises with AHSS stamping parts, as well as the tooling and die manufacturing companies, or part design units, function test units, will have an unexpected harvest.

The conference will provide an opportunity to meet old friends, make new friends, exchange information, share the achievement and look forward to the future.

In addition to academic exchanges, the conference will also invite relevant enterprises to exhibit technology and equipment, and organize some exciting technological and social visits in return for delegates.

We sincerely invite domestic and foreign people around the world to actively participate in the second international conference on AHSS and press hardening.

Topics

Authors from the worldwide are welcome to gather at this international academic convention. The conference will collect 200 papers and all accepted papers will be indexed by ISTP, Google Scholar, the details can be seen via conference website www.ICHSU.org. About 72 accepted papers will be reported by oral, others will be posters.

Advanced High Strength Steel,Modeling & Simulation,AHSS Products & Application,Industrial Processes,Surface Condition, Friction & Lubrication,Coating & Corrosion,Process and Industry Production Process Design,Press Hardening of AHSS,Cold Forming of Tailor-welded Blank,Hot Stamping of Aluminum Alloy,Heating & Cooling,Microstructure & Phase Transformation,Tools & Dies,Testing & Assessment,Hot stamping Parts Design,Laser Cutting and Welding,Others
