Registration

Registration fees will include conference materials, banquet, lunch, tea breaks, etc. Transportation and accommodation are at your own expense.

 

Registration fee for authors: 2,500 CNY (400 USD) per person

 

  • Discount for the second paper of an author: 2,000 CNY (or 350 USD) per person

  • Discount for non-first authors of a paper: 1,500 CNY (or 250 USB) per person

  • Discount for students: 2,000 CNY (or 350 USD) per person

  • Extra fees for extra pages if over 5 pages: 200 CNY (or 35 USD) per page

 

Registration fee for non-authors: 3,800 CNY (or 600 USD) per person

 

 

Exhibition rate: 15,800 CNY (or 2,500 USD) per booth

 

  • Include: 2 normal audience seats, an uniform exhibition booth.

  • Exhibition Scope: Hot stamping products; Hot stamping system integrated production line; Equipment including heating equipment, transportation equipment, laser equipment, presses, robots, dies; System and accessories including 3D simulation software, robot system integration, sensor, vision system; Materials including high strength steel, coating materials, die steel.
