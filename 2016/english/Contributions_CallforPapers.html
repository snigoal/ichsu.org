Topics:

Authors from the worldwide are welcome to gather at this international academic convention. The conference will collect 200 papers and all accepted papers will be indexed by ISTP, Google Scholar, the details can be seen via conference website www.ichsu.org. About 84 accepted papers will be reported by oral, others will be posters.After the conference, outstanding papers will be published based on the following topics, which are included but not limited:

  • • Advanced High Strength Steel/Aluminum Alloy
  • • Hot Stamping of AHSS
  • • Cold/Hot Forming of Tailor-welded Blank
  • • Cold/Hot Forming of Tailor-welded Blank
  • • Modeling & Simulation
  • • Products & Application
  • • Surface Condition, Friction & Lubrication
  • • Coating, Corrosion & Preservation
  • • Process and Industry Production Process Design
  • • Heating & Cooling
  • • Microstructure & Phase Transformation
  • • Tools & Dies Design and Manufacture
  • • Testing & Assessment
  • • Hot stamping Parts Design of Auto Body
  • • Laser Cutting and Welding
  • • Digital and Intelligent Technology of Hot Stamping Equipment and its Application
  • • Others
